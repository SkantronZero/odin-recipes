Leftover Ham Croque Monsieur

Ingredients

  • Leftover Ham
  • French Roll
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Gouda

Instructions

Make the bechamel

  1. Brown butter in pan over medium low heat
  2. Add equal part flour to butter
  3. Stir until completely incorporated
  4. Slowly add milk and stir continuously
  5. Add gouda in pieces until completely melted

Prepare Sandwich

  1. Slice french roll in half long ways
  2. Scoop out extra bread from one side
  3. Fill scooped half with leftover ham
  4. Spread bechamel over other side

Bake

  1. Place sandwich in toaster oven
  2. With sandwich face open, bake for 4 minutes at 375 degrees F
  3. Toast with sandwich face open for 2 minutes
